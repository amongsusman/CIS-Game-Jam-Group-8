Core Systems

How You Solve It

Stand on Giant

Tiny can jump and land directly on Giant's head to reach otherwise inaccessible platforms and elevated switches.

Teamwork
Throw Crates

Giant lifts wooden crates, charges a throw, and launches them onto pressure plates — sometimes across the entire level.

Physics
Lava Shield

Giant hoists a heavy metal crate overhead. The crate blocks falling magma drips, letting Tiny safely walk through the hazard zone.

Hazard
Locked Levers

Some switches only unlock when Giant stands on a pressure plate. Coordination is key — one player holds, the other acts.

Puzzles
Sci-Fi Gun

Tiny picks up a plasma gun to shoot moving target switches. Hold Q to charge and aim the trajectory arc before firing.

Precision
Lava Boats

Both players ride a wooden boat across molten lava. Giant's weight inside the hull steers it — push against walls to propel across.

One keyboard.
Two minds.
Zero margin for error.

Tiny & Giant is a shared-keyboard co-op game. Both players must be present, communicating, and trusting each other to escape the volcano. Every puzzle is designed so neither player can succeed alone.

Start Together
Quick Start

Get Into The Volcano
In 60 Seconds

1
Sit Together

Both players share one keyboard. Player 1 uses WASD, Player 2 uses the Arrow Keys. No controllers, no downloads.

2
Read the Room

Survey each level together. Spot the door, find the switches, and figure out what each player's role will be before moving.

3
Coordinate

Most puzzles require precise timing — one player holds a plate while the other cranks a lever. Communicate or restart.

4
Reach the Door

Both players must enter the exit door simultaneously. An iris wipe confirms your escape and advances to the next sector.
